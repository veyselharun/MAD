## Dart Overview

**Dart:** An approachable, portable, and productive programming language for high-quality apps on any platform. A client-optimized language for developing fast apps on any platform.

**Technical envelope:** The choices made during development that shape the capabilities and strengths of a language.

**Dart's technical envelope:** Particularly suited to client development, prioritizing both development (sub-second stateful hot reload) and high-quality production experiences across a wide variety of compilation targets (web, mobile, and desktop).

Dart is the foundation of Flutter.
